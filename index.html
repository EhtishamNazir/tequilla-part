A bit about the creator
TequilaParty was founded by technology industry veteran Jameson Huckaba. Jameson is a long time crypto and blockchain enthusiast from the early days of Bitcoin, and has spent time working on projects in the areas of Aerospace, Machine Learning/AI, and Remote Sensing. Jameson has also spent significant time in the beverage industry, and is certified by the Mexican Government's Consejo Regulador Del Tequila (CRT), Tequila's regulatory agency.
